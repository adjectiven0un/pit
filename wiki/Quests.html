Quests
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Like every Hypixel game, the Pit provides players with Quests that can be completed for Hypixel experience and gold.

The Hypixel Pit offers three quests:
  • Daily Quest: Hunter: Kill 25 players. Rewards: 3000 Hypixel Experience, 1000 Gold
  • Daily Quest: Contractor: Complete one contract. Rewards: 3000 Hypixel Experience, 1000 Gold
  • Weekly Quest: Double Up! Earn 10000 gold from any source excluding trades. Rewards: 7000 Hypixel Experience, 10000 Gold
