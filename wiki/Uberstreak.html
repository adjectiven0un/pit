Uberstreak
From the Hypixel Pit Wiki
Uberstreak is a megastreak in the Hypixel Pit. It activates upon the player reaching 100 kills.
While the megastreak is active, the Uberstreak megastreak has one perk:
  • Gain +50% chance to find mystic items from kills.
As you earn kills during an Uberstreak, your ability to get kills and stay alive becomes progressively worse.
  • At 100 kills, activate Uberstreak. Deal 40% less damage to players with no prestige.
  • At 200 kills, also lose 2â™¥ max health.
  • At 300 kills, potion effects last half as long as they normally do.
  • At 400 kills, you can no longer heal.
After the player dies, if they had at least 400 kills, they will receive an Uberdrop.

Uberdrops can contain:
  • 5-15 Philosopher's Cactus
  • 1-2 Funky Feather
  • 1 Hidden Jewel Sword
  • 1 Totally Legit Gem
Unlike most megastreaks, there is a limit to rewards earned from Uberstreak. Every day, you may only earn an Uberdrop four times, costing:
  • First Uberstreak daily: Free
  • Second Uberstreak daily: 5 renown
  • Third Uberstreak daily: 10 renown
  • Fourth Uberstreak daily: 15 renown
